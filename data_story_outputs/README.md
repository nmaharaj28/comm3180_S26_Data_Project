### Are the #OscarsSoWhite?: Analyzing Academy Award Demographics and #OscarsSoWhite Campaign Efficacy (Final_Blog.ipynb)

The blog opens with an introduction to the #OscarsSoWhite movement and the project's four research questions and hypotheses, then presents findings across three analytical sections — Oscar nomination and winner demographics by gender and race across 98 ceremonies, box office performance of nominated films split by lead race and era, and sentiment analysis and timeline of #OscarsSoWhite tweets from 2015–2022 — before synthesizing the results into the project's emergent story. Sections 3 and 4 are currently in progress and will be finalized before final submission.
